Description

Tetris is a puzzle game from the Soviet Union that tests your reflexes. Seven different shaped blocks fall down the game field one after the other. The objective of the game is to keep the blocks from piling up to the top of the game field. To do this, you can move the blocks to the left and right, and rotate them as they fall. If you completely fill in one horizontal line, that line disappears and you get points. If the blocks pile up to the top of the game field, then that's the end of the game.
